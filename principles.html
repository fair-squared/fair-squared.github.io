FAIR²: Principles and Key Features

  • Findable: Comprehensive metadata ensures data is easily discoverable by both humans and machines.
  • Accessible: Data is made available under clear, transparent access conditions, including necessary authentication and authorization mechanisms.
  • Interoperable: Standardized formats and protocols enable seamless data integration and exchange across different systems and platforms.
  • Reusable: Data is accompanied by detailed documentation, including provenance, licensing, and methods for reproducibility and responsible use.
  • AI-Ready (AIR): Structured and enriched data ensures it can seamlessly integrate into AI and machine learning workflows.
  • Responsible AI (RAI): Ethical guidelines are embedded in data handling, ensuring transparency, accountability, and bias mitigation in AI applications.
