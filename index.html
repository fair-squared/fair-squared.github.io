Welcome to FAIR²

FAIR² builds on the FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable and Reusable) principles by ensuring data is also:

  • AI-Ready (AIR): Optimized for AI and machine learning applications.
  • Responsible AI (RAI) Aligned: Managed with ethical and equitable practices, ensuring bias reduction and data privacy.
  • Contextualized: Enriched with the context and metadata needed for better understanding and reuse.
  • Ethical, Sustainable, and Equitable: Promoting long-term, inclusive, and responsible research.

FAIR² prepares data for the future of research—AI-ready, responsibly managed, and impactful.

Why Choose FAIR²?

FAIR² enhances data beyond the FAIR principles by integrating AI-readiness, ethical AI alignment, and essential context. These features ensure that your data is not only reusable but drives scientific advancements. Here's how FAIR² benefits you:

  • Maximize Impact: Enhance the visibility and usability of your data, increasing citations and global recognition.
  • Boost Innovation: Enable AI-driven analysis, transforming your data into powerful tools for new discoveries.
  • Support Ethical AI: Uphold the highest standards of responsible data use with Responsible AI practices.
  • Ensure Global Accessibility: Promote sustainability and equity by making your data accessible across the research community.
