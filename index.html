Welcome to FAIR²

In today's fast-paced world of AI-driven research, the data we produce needs to be more than just accessible—it must be AI-ready, responsibly managed, and impactful. FAIR² (or FAIR SQUARED) combines the power of AI-readiness with responsible and transparent data governance, accelerating the future of science.

Why FAIR²?

FAIR² builds on the success of the FAIR principles, providing a framework to manage scientific data that is discoverable, accessible, interoperable, reusable, and AI-ready. Here’s why you should make your data FAIR²:

  • Maximize Impact: Ensure your data is reusable and AI-ready, boosting global visibility and citations.
  • Encourage Collaboration: Break down barriers and open your data to interdisciplinary research across the globe.
  • Responsible AI: Ensure ethical data usage with Responsible AI guidelines built into the framework.
  • Sustainability & Equity: Promote research equality and reduce data redundancy, fostering global sustainability.
