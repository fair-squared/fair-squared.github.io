What is FAIR²?

FAIR², developed by SENSCIENCE, is an open, validatable specification designed to advance open, AI-ready, and responsibly managed data. It enhances the traditional FAIR principles (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable) by incorporating AI-readiness and Responsible AI (RAI) components, ensuring data is prepared for AI-driven research while meeting ethical standards.

At its core, FAIR² is built on a SHACL schema that provides validation and structure. It integrates standards such as schema.org, the Croissant Metadata Format for detailed metadata, PROV Ontology for provenance tracking, and QUDT for standardized units. These components ensure data remains interoperable and reusable across various scientific disciplines, while being optimized for AI workflows.

By adding context and responsible data practices, FAIR² enables researchers to maximize the impact of their data. Whether in environmental science, health, or computational research, FAIR² ensures that data can be shared and reused with transparency and efficiency, facilitating more advanced scientific insights.

FAIR² represents the next generation of reusable scientific data, merging the foundational FAIR principles with cutting-edge AI best practices. The FAIR² logo, inspired by the traditional Japanese hanko (判子) seal, features a red square symbolizing trust and integrity. Built on established standards from schema.org, W3C, and MLCommons, FAIR² ensures data is AI-ready, enriched with methodological and contextual details, and aligned with Responsible AI principles.
